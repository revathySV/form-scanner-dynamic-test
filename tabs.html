Tab-revealed form

Two tabs. Tab 1 "Overview" shows plain text. Tab 2 "Contact" contains a form, but the panel is hidden on load. Pre-CN-3064 the scanner sees the form as not-visible and filters it out. Post-CN-3064 the tab pass clicks [role="tab"] elements and reveals the form with discoveryMethod: "tab_reveal".
