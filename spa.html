SPA route — contact form behind client-side route

The "Contact" button below uses history.pushState to change the URL without a full navigation. The form only renders after the route change fires. Pre-CN-3064 the scanner's waitForNavigation times out and the form is missed. Post-CN-3064 the pushState hook catches the route change and emits the form with discoveryMethod: "spa_route".
