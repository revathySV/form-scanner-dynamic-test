3-step wizard

Each submit reveals the next step. The wizard uses role="progressbar" + aria-valuenow + "Step N of M" text, which is exactly what the CN-3064 wizard detector looks for. Pre-CN-3064 the scanner only captures step 1. Post-CN-3064 all three steps are emitted, each tagged multi_step_child with incrementing stepIndex and a parentFormHash link back to step 1.
