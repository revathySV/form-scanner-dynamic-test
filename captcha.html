CAPTCHA-gated contact form

The form below is gated by a simulated reCAPTCHA widget. The .g-recaptcha class and [data-sitekey] attribute match the worker's CAPTCHA detector. Before CN-3017 the scanner would fill and submit, the submit would deterministically fail, and the form would be reported as a submit failure. After CN-3017 the scanner detects the gate up-front and emits submitSkipReason: "captcha_gated" with status submit_skipped — not a failure.
