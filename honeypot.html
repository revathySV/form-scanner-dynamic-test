Honeypot-trapped contact form

The form below includes a hidden <input name="website"> positioned off-screen — a classic anti-spam honeypot. Humans don't see it. Pre-CN-3017 the scanner would fill it (because it's an <input>) and the server would silently mark the submission as spam. Post-CN-3017 the scanner matches the suspicious name + hidden visibility and skips the field, logging it in honeypotsSkipped[].
